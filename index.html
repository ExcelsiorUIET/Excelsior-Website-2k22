F.A.Q

  • What type of events will be there?

    Excelsior’22 will consist of both technical and cultural events including a star night on the last day of the event.

  • When is the event?

    Excelsior’22 will be held on the 12th and 13th of May.

  • What is the exact location?

    Cultural events and Star night will be held at Geeta Bhavan and the technical events will take place simultaneously in UIET.

  • What are the parking arrangements and directions?

    Parking will be provided. And, for directions, posters will be put up at various locations in the university.

  • When can I register?

    You can register beforehand using online mode but on-spot registrations are also available.

  • Can I register at the venue?

    Yes, participants can register at the venue as well. The registration desk will be set up at the venue.

  • How do I pay for the registration?

    The payment can be done using both online and offline methods. The online payment can be done from the registration section of the website.

  • Are there any age restrictions for registration?

    No, there is no such age restriction on participation. Although, participants must be currently studying in the institution.
