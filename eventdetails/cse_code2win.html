Codathon

Caution: Not for Muggles

If you are a coding geek, this contest is for you guyz! UIET Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra has come up with Codathon where coders can truly showcase their skills and can also learn a lot from this competitive environment.
The coding contest, Codathon, will be open for all years but the difficulty level will vary accordingly. Complexity will be the same for first and second & third and fourth year.

A. Phase 1 [Technical Quiz]
  1. This Phase is basically based on Programming, Data Structures, DBMS, Computer Networks, Operating System, etc.
  2. The Top Participants will be shortlisted for the next round.
  3. This Phase consists of 20-30 Questions and the given time is 30 minutes.
  4. Any Coding Platform will be used for conduction (Ex: Hackerrank, Hackerearth, D2C competitions, etc)
B. Phase 2 [Coding Round]
  1. Only the shortlisted candidates of Phase-1 will be eligible for this round.
  2. 2-3 problems will be provided to be solved in 1 hr.
  3. Languages [Python, C, C++, Java, JavaScript]
  4. The Winner will be the one who scores best in both the phases.
  5. The top two performers will get a Cash Prize and some Goodies*.
  6. Any Coding Platform will be used for conduction (Ex: Hackerrank, Hackerearth, D2C competitions, etc.
