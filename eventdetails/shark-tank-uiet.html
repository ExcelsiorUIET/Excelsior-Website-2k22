Shark Tank UIET

Start in public

Description

EXCELSIOR2k22 in collaboration with E-Cell UIET KUK is organizing an entrepreneurship event, SHARK TANK UIET. It gives a wonderful opportunity to candidates to explore within themselves and showcase their startup idea to an ever wider audience and judges, testing their talent for devising a suitable idea and presenting it thoroughly in the allotted time. It will help develop entrepreneurship skills among future engineer-entrepreneurs.

Rules:

Eligibility: Open for all.

Participation in this competition can be either individually or in a group.

Maximum time allotted for presentation will be 5-7 minutes.

Prototype of the idea/concept can be presented if possible. Idea should be original. Plagiarism will not be tolerated.

The pitch submission will take place three days prior to the event.

All participants must ensure that they have a ready business plan for their start-up.

After the pitch, a questioning round will take place where the audience, as well as the judges, would question the participants on their start-up.

Date time venue:

  • Registration: Till 7 May 2022
  • Timeline: Registration-Pitch Submission-Final Presentation-Result
  • Registration charges are ____ rupees per (team/candidate)
  • Mode -

    • Judgement criteria: Business Model, Scalability, Team.

    Note that at any point in time the latest information will be that which is on the website. However, the registered participants will be informed through mail about any changes on the website.
