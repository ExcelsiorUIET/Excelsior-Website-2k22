Jab We Dance

Dance your soul away

Description

Lace up your dancing shoes to match your steps with thousands of heart beats as EXCELSIOR 2K22 brings the grand dance competition inviting solo and group performances.

Rules:

This competition is for solo or team participation consisting of maximum of 20 members.

The maximum time limit for the performance is 5-8 minutes.

No flammable objects are allowed during the performance.

The screening will be conducted before the performance.

Participants are directed to bring their instruments.

No use of obscene or offensive words during the performance.

A brief introduction is to be given regarding the costumes, music, and content to be performed before the performance to the event managers present there.

Coordination of team lead with our teams is necessary.

Multiple entries per college are allowed.

Any change made on the spot will not be argued against.

If you are not present at the given time, you will be disqualified.

Negative marks will be awarded if the time limit exceeds.
