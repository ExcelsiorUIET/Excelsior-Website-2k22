About Thinkathon

A Problem Statement will be Provided based on some advancement in UIET, KU.

This is a team based competition.

At Least one female participant is mandatory.

A team will consist of at least two members and at most four.

A. Phase 1 [Prototype submission]

Basically In this phase a team has to submit the layout(of solution) and prototype according to the problem statement.

Prototype may include any UI design of the solution to have a clear idea. The teams with the best ideas (solutions) will be shortlisted for the next round.

B. Phase 2 [Round table dicussion]

Only the shortlisted candidates of Phase-1 will be eligible for this round.

Finalists will be given an opportunity to present their solutions to the eminent jury panel.

This Phase is basically to test students' way of describing any idea, clarity about the problem, its solution, and novelty itof the idea.

The two teams with the best solutions will be the winners.

The winners will get a cash prize and some goodies.


Note: This is open for everyone
