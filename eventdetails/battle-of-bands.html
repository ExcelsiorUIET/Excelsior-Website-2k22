Battle of Bands

Drum-Drum Diga-Diga

Description

ROCK ONN!!! This is DRUM-CHARADES of Excelsior 2K22 where there will be gazab ka crowd and an aura full of youthfulness and you can be the showstopper by setting up the stage on fire with your thundering band and also compete with others like you in this battle of bands! So be ready with your guitar and drums and come to THE BATTLE OF BANDS!

Guidelines:

Crew members should range from 7-9.

The screening will be conducted.

Participants are asked to bring their instruments.

No use of obscene or offensive words.

Judges' decisions will be the ultimate decision and won't be argued over.

A brief introduction is required before or during the screening of the content you are about to perform. Costumes and music need to be informed about before the performance. Coordination of leads of various teams is necessary.

Multiple entries per college are allowed.

Any change made on the spot will not be argued against. If you are not present at the given time, you will be disqualified.

You are eligible for negative marking if you go over the time limit.
