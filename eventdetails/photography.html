"Ek SNAPSTREAK ki keemat tum kya jaano Ramesh Babu?"
Because that daily streak made everyone a photographer!! Now here comes your time, UIET brings a Photography Competition in Excelsior 2K22 where you can show your talent and grab awards and goodies. Just hold your camera and click best pictures having some meaning hidden in them and label them with a nice caption and rock the show!!!

Rulebook:
  1. Contestants must carry their own camera/mobile phone.
  2. No editing is allowed.
  3. You are required to provide a unique title & description for each image submitted.
  4. Image Specifications: Image format: JPG/ JEPG/ PNG.
  5. Entries will be judged equally on creativity, technical expertise and aesthetic value.
