Beyond the आवाज़

For your talent to get wider recognition

Description

Set your notes to strike notes with ever wider audience and compete amongst best of the contemporaries in singing organised by EXCELSIOR 2K22 on 12th May, 2022.

Guidelines:

The time duration of the performance is 2-4 minutes.

The time to set up the instruments is 2 minutes maximum.

The screening will be conducted.

Participants are asked to bring their own instruments.

No use of obscene or offensive words.

Judges' decisions will be the ultimate decision and won’t be argued over.

A brief introduction is required before or during the screening of the content you are about to perform.

Costumes and music need to be informed about before the performance.

Coordination of leads of various teams is necessary.

Multiple entries per college are not allowed.

Any change made on the spot will not be argued against.

If you are not present at the given time, you will be disqualified.

You are eligible for negative marking if you go over the time limit.
