Short Film Making

A Lifetime in Minutes

Rulebook:
  1. Entries for the Short Film Festival are open for everyone.
  2. The Short Film must be based on the topics provided.
  3. The short film can be in any language. However, all films must include subtitles in English.
  4. The total runtime of the Short Film, including opening & closing credits, should strictly be 7-8 minutes.
  5. Judges' decisions will be final and binding.
  6. Please adhere to the theme.
