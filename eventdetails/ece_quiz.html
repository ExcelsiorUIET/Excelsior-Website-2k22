Quiz

Unlock your knowledge at the speed of light

This quiz Competition is aimed to bring out the best in candidates and recognize their true potential. We are working to motivate the candidates to explore the branch of Electronics and Communication engineering. It strives to popularize academic competition and assist the development of the learning spirit among candidates.

Rulebook:
  1. This quiz will be open to everyone i.e. any year student can participate but the complexity will vary accordingly which will be the same for first & second years and third & fourth years.
  2. This competition is for individual participation.
  3. This contest will consist of 20/25 questions.
  4. The theme of the competition will be Core Electronics. It is basically based on the prior knowledge and basics of Electronics and Communication engineering
  5. The total time will be 60 minutes.
  6. If any contestant found using any device to use internet or doing any false practice he/she shall be disqualified without any hesitation to ensure a fair competition.
  7. Each contestant must have his/ her ID card.
