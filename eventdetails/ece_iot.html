IN-O-VATIVE THING

UIET, Kurukshetra University presents an INTERNET OF THINGS (IoT) competition to enhance the understanding and awareness of various IoT technologies.

Problem Statements:
  1. Smart Farms
  2. IOT mining, tracking and worker safety helmet
  3. Smart health monitoring system
  4. Smart Dustbin
  5. Smart Parking
  6. All SIH problem statements involving IOT(hardware) will be considered.
Rulebook:
  1. Eligibility: Open for all students.
  2. This competition is for team participation consisting of up to 6 members.
  3. Structure of contest: In this competition, a team, by selecting one topic out of 6 topics, will need to build a device or demonstrate its functionality if you have pre build and each team will be given 15 minutes to explain their project and show the working prototype.
  4. Total duration of the event will be 3 hours.
Requirements
  1. Each team must have at least one laptop of their own.
  2. Each member of team must have his/her ID card.
