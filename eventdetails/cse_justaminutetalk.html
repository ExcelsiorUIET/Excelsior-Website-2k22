About Swift Radio

  • Registration is compulsory for everyone.

  • You’ll be given three random burning topics and you have to choose one.

  • One minute will be provided to each and every participant to speak on a given topic.

  • You’ll be having 30 seconds to think about what to deliver in that one minute.

  • Judging criteria:
      Deviation
      Repetition
      Hesitation

  • Clarity and knowledge of topic and ideas presentation

  • The total time duration of the challenge will be 30 minutes

  • The winner will get cash prize and some goodies.
