Soft words win hard hearts

EXCELSIOR 2K22 gives you the chance of showcasing your speaking skills and at the same time showing your ability to think for the topic and expressing it at that very moment. It will also boost confidence of candidates to speak amongst large crowds promptly.

Rulebook:
  1. Eligibility: Open for all.
  2. This competition is for individual participation.
  3. The Participants will have to draw a chit from a box and speak on the topic mentioned on it.
  4. Provision of 2 minutes will be given to prepare for the mentioned topic.
  5. The Time allotted to speak will be 3-4 minutes.
  6. Negative Marks would be awarded for extra time.
  7. The Topic will not be changed once the chit is drawn by the participant.
  8. No objection regarding the varying complexity of fellow participants' topic will be entertained.
  9. Provision of on-the-spot registration is also provided.
