CLASH OF BOTS

Only the mighty shall survive

This event is aimed to boost the confidence as well as raise the bar of skills and knowledge of candidates in the field of Robotics.

Rulebook:
  1. Eligibility: Open for everyone.
  2. This competition is for team participation consisting of 3-5 members.
  3. Structure of contest:
    One-to-one battle: Only two robots will be there in the ring at a time. If the robot gets crashed or runs out of the ring, the team will be disqualified. The time duration for this event will be 1.5 hours.
    Many robots in a ring at one time: In this scenario, the aim should be to push the opponentâ€™s robot off the area boundary to score maximum points.
  4. It is necessary for each member of the team to register.
  5. Judgment criteria: There will be three rounds, the team that wins the maximum rounds will win the competition.
  6. Requirements: Robots must be self-made. If something is found wrong with the robots that team will be disqualified. The weight of Robots should not exceed 3 kg. Dimensions of the Robot must be within 300 x 300 mm. The participants must bring their Id proof with them.
