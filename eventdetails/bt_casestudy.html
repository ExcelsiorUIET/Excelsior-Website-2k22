Case Study

Improving the world one problem at a time

Case studies include an in-depth understanding and research on an issue to devise feasible and practical solutions. It provides individuals with critical thinking standards and clarity on various issues.
This competition to Biotech related problem statements will be decided which will be announced to the teams beforehand the team must then make a 10 minute presentation in dealing with the problem and present it before the judges panel.

Rulebook:
  1. The team can consist of maximum of four members.
  2. Multiple teams from the same colleges are allowed to participate in the event.
  3. The participating teams must bring all of their electronic gadgets like laptops, phones , etc. on which they will showcase their presentation.
  4. Any kind of plagiarism or any explicit content in the presentation shall not be entertained at any cost and the participants will be disqualified.
  5. Participants must adhere strictly to the time allotted for each presentation which is 10 minutes.
  6. After the presentation is over the participating teams will then be subjected to a five minute question answer sessions with the judges where they will be answerable to any doubts and queries that the judges might have.
  7. In case of any dispute/discrepancy the decision shall rest with the organizing committee which shall be final and binding no further queries in this regard will be entertained.
