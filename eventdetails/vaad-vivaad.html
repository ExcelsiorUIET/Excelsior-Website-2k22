Vaad Vivaad

Speak openly and debate honestly

Description

EXCELSIOR 2K22 is organizing a debate competition to showcase your ability to keep your opinion amongst masses. This will make-rise the spirit of candidate to further furnish these skills when encountered with best of the talent.

Rules:

Eligibility: Open for all.

This competition is for individual participation.

The Topic will be given on-the-spot.

Provision of 2 minutes is provided to prepare for the given topic.

To introduce your topic and give your viewpoint amongst judges and audience, time of 1-2 minutes would be allotted.

Provision of 30 seconds is provided for counter-attack and cross-questioning which can only be used for 2-3 times during the contest.

At the end, time of 1 minute is provided to conclude the topic.

Opinions should be kept up-to-the point, no need to exaggerate your point.

Negative marks would be awarded for extra time.

Use of offensive language and violent behaviour will be entertained strictly and will lead to immediate disqualification.
